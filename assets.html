Scaffolding

Our goal was to create a scaffolding structure that was simplified down to a few basic structures, halfs, thirds, and quarters. Often times scaffolding helpers are too intensive and this is an attempt to have truely an utilitarian scaffolding based simply on our needs.

Use as paragraph scaffolding

<div class="container">
  <div class="row">
    <div class="half"> 
      <div class="inner"> ... </div>
    </div>
    <div class="quarter">
       <div class="inner"> ... </div>
    </div>
    <div class="quarter">
       <div class="inner"> ... </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

Simple layout scaffolding

The scaffolding can also be used as a simple layout tool. In this case just use the container and the associated spacer, leaving out the row wrapper and the inner div. 

<div class="container">
  <div class="half"> ... </div>
  <div class="half"> ... </div>   
</div>
